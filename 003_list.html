HOW TO COOK MY FAVORITE MEAL


BEANS AND PLANTAIN


INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups of brown beans
  • 2 riped plantains (peeled and sliced)
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2-3 cooking spoons of palm oil
  • 2 teaspoon of ground fresh pepper
  • salt to taste
  • water

STEPS

  1. wash and boil the beans in a pot with enough water. cook on medium heat until soft (about 45 minutes to 1 hour).
  2. add chopped onions and allow to boil with the beans for extra flavor.
  3. once the beans are almost soft, add sliced plantain.
  4. add palmoil,pepper,and salt.
  5. let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until the oil is well blended and the meal is thick and soft.
  6. stir and serve hot.

SUMMARY

Beans and plantain is a hearty, nitritious meal that's both delicious and filling.

It's easy to prepare and great for breakfast,lunch or dinner.

The sweet softness of the plantain pairs perfectly with the richness of the beans
